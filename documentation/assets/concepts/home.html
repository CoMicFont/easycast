Main Easycast Concepts

To get started and use Easycast's full potential, you need to understand a few concepts first

Cast

A cast is a composition of assets (images, html pages, video, etc.). Assets are stacked on top of each other, e.g. to let you use multiple images with transparency.

Scene

A scene is a collection of casts, each projected on a particular display. A scene is therefore the coherent exposition of various images and text on the available displays.

Display

Displays are virtual screens. Each display has a unique number and unique web address and presents a particular cast of a scene at every moment.
